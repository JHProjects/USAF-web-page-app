About the festival


Újezd Street Art Festival is an anual Open air exhibition.
usual content consists of popular czech and europe based street artists.
Theme of the festival spins around grafitti, murals, street instalations and sculptures


Under the auspices of MUDr. Bohuslav Svoboda, Mayor of Prague
